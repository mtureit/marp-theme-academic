## About Me

### My Background and Research

- Master's student at **Nagaoka University of Technology**
- Research: **An AI system to support high school students in choosing their careers**
  - Technology: SGA (Simulation with Generative Agents)
  - Students fill out a form → AI suggests personalized career paths

<div class="two-columns">
  <div class="column">

### How the system works
- Reads each student's interests and values
- Suggests career paths from age 18 to 30
- Students choose the ideas they like ✅

  </div>
  <div class="column">

- **245 students** at one high school participated

## The Problem I Want to Solve

### Career Education in Japan is Not Equal ⚠️

**Students in rural areas have less support**
- Small schools have fewer teachers and resources
- Career counselors are often not available
- Students from rural areas tend to have lower university enrollment rates

**Teachers don't have enough time**
- One teacher handles 30–40 students
- Individual career advice is almost impossible
- Many students graduate without a clear picture of their future

<div class="bubble">
<span class="marker">Where you grow up</span> should not decide the quality of your career education
</div>

## What I Can Do ①

### Expand the System to More Schools

**Short-term goals:**
- Test the system at more schools, especially **rural schools** 🏫
