Eita MATSUURA

## About Me

### My Research: AI for Career Education

- I study **how AI can help high school students** choose their future careers
- I use a technology called **SGA (Simulation with Generative Agents)**
  - Students answer questions about their interests and values
  - AI reads the answers and suggests career paths for them

<div class="two-columns">
  <div class="column">

### How the system works
- Students write about themselves
- AI generates career path ideas
- Students save or skip each idea

  </div>
  <div class="column">

### What I found
- 245 high school students tried it
- About **1 in 3** AI suggestions were accepted

  </div>
</div>

## 1. Social Background

### People Are Getting Lonelier and Lonelier

- **Loneliness is a serious social problem** 🚨
  - The U.S. Surgeon General called loneliness "like a disease" (2023)
  - Young people (age 18–34) feel the loneliest

- **Dating apps are very popular (300+ million users worldwide), but...**
  - Many people still can't find a good partner

- **AI is already part of our daily life**
  - AI assistants and generative AI are now normal for us

<div class="bubble">
CNN (2024): About <span class="marker">1 in 3 young men</span> say they have dated an AI
</div>

---

## 1. Social Background

### What Is an LLM?

**LLM = Large Language Model**
- An AI that learned from billions of sentences
- It can talk, answer, and explain — like a human
- Examples: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini

**Why can LLMs be used for human relationships?**

| Point | Old AI | LLM |
|-------|--------|-----|
| Talking style | Fixed answers | Natural conversation |
| Understanding you | Not much | Can read your feelings |
| Emotion | None | Understands context |

---

## 2. Problem to be Solved

### The Problem with Today's Dating Apps ⚠️
- Apps match people by **age, hobbies, and photos** — this has been the same for 20+ years
- But research says: **real compatibility comes from conversation, not profiles**

### What Actually Happens
- Someone looks perfect on the app, but when you talk... it feels different
- Many people quit the app because of this

<div class="bubble">
We need a system that checks compatibility <span class="marker">through real conversation</span>
</div>

---

## 2. Problem to be Solved

### Misunderstandings and Fights Are Very Common
- The #1 reason couples break up is miscommunication
- Professional counseling costs a lot of money 💸

### People Cannot See Their Own Patterns
- "Why do we always fight about the same thing?"
- Hard to see from the inside

### AI Partners Create New Questions ⚠️
- Is it okay to replace human love with AI?

---

## 3. Technical Solution

### Idea ①: "Talk First, Then Match"

```
Person A ──┐
           ├──► Chat through LLM ──► Compatibility score ──► Match!
Person B ──┘
```

**Steps:**
1. Two people have a conversation with LLM as the host
2. LLM watches their talking style, emotions, and response speed
3. Compatibility score is calculated from **real interaction**

<div class="bubble">
Measure compatibility from <span class="marker">real conversation</span>, not just a profile
</div>

---

## 3. Technical Solution

### Idea ②: AI Analyzes Couples' Conversations

- A couple gives their LINE or chat history to AI
- LLM finds patterns in their arguments

**What LLM finds:** 🔍
- "You two always fight about this topic"
- "One person talks too much, the other can't speak"

**Then LLM suggests better ways to talk:**
- Rewrites the conversation in a healthier way
- Shows examples of good communication ✅

## 4. Effect of the Solution

### Good Effects

**For finding a partner:**
- Better matches — based on real conversation, not just looks

**For couples:** 🤝
- Cheap and easy relationship support
- Catch problems early before they get big

**For lonely people:** 💬
- A safe place to practice social skills

---

## 4. Effect of the Solution

### Problems and Concerns

### Privacy Risk 🔒
- Relationship data is the most personal information
- Current laws (like GDPR) were NOT made for this kind of AI

### You Might Become Too Dependent on AI
- "I prefer talking to AI than to real people" — this is a real risk

### Big Questions We Don't Have Answers for Yet
- If AI gives bad advice and a relationship breaks, who is responsible?
